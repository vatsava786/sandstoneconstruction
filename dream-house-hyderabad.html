We have faced adversaries that we have never thought of. The lockdown forced us to stay at our rented place. Many of the families are at homes while the bread winner of the family stays far just because the rented flats aren’t comfortable for everyone to stay in. This quarantine taught that staying with family is the only solace we are ever going to find. But staying with restrictions on almost everything has made people reconsider their options of having home.

We, at Sandstone constructions, have analysed and understood that the main constraint forcing people to rent is the fear of leaving the dent in their wallet and the never-ending works to build. Our works and timing have always been promising for our customers and are glad to have constructed their dream home by us. We understand the dreams and expectations that revolved around your abode. That is the reason we have push our boundaries from working as an employee in AC to being a entrepreneur builder in scorching sun. All this hard work has been planting trust in people since 2001.



Before designing the infrastructure, we let the consumer give us every precise detail of their dream place. And then we provide them with the blueprint layout of the building. Every doubt about the construction, every detail asked is given to the consumers to maintain transparency of materials. The work is done efficient and fast; the clients are handed over the keys at the earliest. The designs of our constructions are contemporary and are modernized, so you won’t miss out any of the latest features. You wouldn’t even have to contact a third party for furnishing as we also provide with residential interiors. You will walk into your dream home so fast that you couldn’t recollect a day you sat waiting for.
